A small fly accidentally enters an apartment through an open window. Navigate through the apartment, avoid indoor hazards, and find your way back outside.

## Details

- **Genre:** Puzzle / Platformer
- **Engine:** Godot
- **Platform:** Browser (HTML5)
- **Made for:** Monthly ZenoJam

## Controls

- **WASD** — Move
- **Hold Space** — Charge jump, aim with mouse, release to jump
- **Controller:** Left Stick, A button, Right Stick
